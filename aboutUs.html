About Us

For so long, we hadn’t come across an Armenian bakery. Until now.

We wanted to create a business that would put a smile on your face - because we care about you.

Always Fresh

Our mission is to add our culture to this diverse community and bring people of all ethnicities together.

We’d like to offer something different than what the community is used to because we are proud of our heritage and want you to be a part of it.

Scrumptious pastries, artisan breads, and Armenian favorites - you’ll simply find them irresistible. We want you coming back for more. Because we are good at what we do.

We’re telling you, it's worth the calories.
